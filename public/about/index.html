How to play

Each match runs five rounds of deliberation. You may /ask a seat, /defend the council against a seat, /expel a seat (twice per match), or /call_vote when you're ready. At the vote, click a seat on the council map — or arrow-key + Enter — to accuse.

Type /help in-match for the full command list.
